Scratch World

Embark on an exhilarating race through the wild with Jungle Jog! Choose your favorite animal and dash through vibrant jungle trails. Switch between lanes to dodge obstacles and collect shiny coins. It's an endless runner that keeps you on your toes with every tap.

Key Features:

  • - Choose from a variety of adorable animals
  • - Simple tap controls for easy lane switching
  • - Dodge obstacles and collect coins
  • - Stunning jungle-themed graphics
  • - Endless fun and challenging gameplay
